\subsection{Detailed Description}
This class can combine instances of \mbox{\hyperlink{}{X\+Y\+Dataset}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{}{O\+H\+L\+C\+Dataset}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{}{Interval\+X\+Y\+Dataset}} together exposing the union of all the series under one dataset.

\begin{DoxyRefDesc}{Deprecated}
\item[\mbox{\hyperlink{deprecated__deprecated000259}{Deprecated}}]As of version 1.\+0.\+13. This class will be removed from J\+Free\+Chart 1.\+2.\+0 onwards. Anyone needing this facility will need to maintain it outside of J\+Free\+Chart. \end{DoxyRefDesc}
