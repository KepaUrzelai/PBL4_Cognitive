\subsection{Detailed Description}
A box-\/and-\/whisker renderer. This renderer requires a \mbox{\hyperlink{}{Box\+And\+Whisker\+Category\+Dataset}} and is for use with the \mbox{\hyperlink{}{Category\+Plot}} class.
